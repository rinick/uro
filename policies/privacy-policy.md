# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** 2026-06-06

Ulugo is an open-source app for opening, editing, saving, and reviewing Go/Weiqi game records, including SGF files. Ulugo Desktop may also allow users to review games with AI using KataGo.

This Privacy Policy explains how the Ulugo open-source project (“Ulugo,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) handles information when you use Ulugo, including the website, web app, desktop app, and related services available at https://ulugo.com.

## Information We Process

Ulugo may process the files and content you choose to open, create, edit, save, import, export, or review. This may include SGF files, game records, comments, annotations, AI review data, file names, and app settings.

If you contact us for support, we may receive your email address, the contents of your message, and any information you choose to include, such as screenshots, error messages, app version, operating system, browser type, crash reports, or log files.

Ulugo does not automatically collect crash reports, error logs, analytics, or usage tracking information.

## Google Drive Access

If you choose to connect Google Drive, Ulugo uses Google Drive access only to let you open files from Google Drive and save files to Google Drive.

Ulugo only accesses files that you choose to open with Ulugo or files that Ulugo creates or saves for you. Ulugo does not scan your Google Drive, does not access unrelated Drive files, and does not use Google Drive data for advertising.

Ulugo may access limited file information such as file ID, file name, MIME type, and file content for the files you choose to use with Ulugo.

Ulugo’s use of information received from Google APIs will follow the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

## KataGo and AI Review

Ulugo Desktop may use KataGo to analyze Go/Weiqi games. Game records and positions may be processed by the local KataGo engine running on your device, or by components needed to provide AI review features.

Unless clearly stated in the app, Ulugo does not upload your game records for AI review and does not use your game records, Google Drive files, or AI review data to train generalized AI or machine learning models.

## How We Use Information

We use information to:

- Provide Ulugo’s file editing and review features.
- Open, display, edit, save, and export files at your request.
- Load and save files through Google Drive when you choose to connect it.
- Provide KataGo AI review features in Ulugo Desktop.
- Save app preferences.
- Respond to support requests.
- Protect the security and reliability of Ulugo.

## Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not sell Google user data.

We do not use Google Drive data for advertising.

We may share information only when necessary to operate Ulugo, provide support, comply with law, prevent abuse, or protect security.

## Local Storage

Ulugo may store settings, recent file information, cached data, authentication tokens, or AI review data on your device.

In the desktop app, authentication tokens may be stored using operating-system-protected storage when available.

## Data Retention

Ulugo keeps information only as long as reasonably needed to provide the app, comply with law, resolve disputes, or maintain security.

Files opened from Google Drive are accessed at your request. Unless you choose to save, upload, or sync a file, Ulugo does not permanently store your Google Drive files on our servers.

Support emails and any information you choose to send us may be kept as long as needed to respond to your request, maintain project records, or protect the project.

## Your Choices

You can use Ulugo without connecting Google Drive where local file features are available.

You can revoke Ulugo’s Google access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

You can delete local app data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app.

You can choose what information to include when contacting us for support.

## Security

We use reasonable measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Children

Ulugo can be used by children, including children under 13, but Ulugo does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Google and its advertising partners may collect or receive information from your browser or device as a result of ad serving on Ulugo’s website. This information is handled by Google and its partners according to their own privacy policies.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
